LifePass UK Privacy Policy

Bundle ID: com.lifepass.uk | Last updated: June 11, 2026

LifePass UK is a paid download app for life organization and personal management. The app is designed to work locally on your device.

Short version:
  • No account is required.
  • No cloud service is used.
  • No subscriptions, in-app purchases, or advertising are included.
  • Your app data stays on your device.
  • We do not upload, track, sell, or share personal data.
  • No third-party SDKs are included.

Data Storage

Any notes, settings, reminders, checklists, calculations, logs, or study progress you create are stored locally on your device using Apple platform storage. The app does not operate a server account system and does not sync your data to a developer-controlled cloud service.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal information through this app. The app does not include analytics, tracking pixels, advertising networks, or third-party SDKs.

Network and Cloud Use

The core app experience is offline-first. The app does not upload your content to our servers and does not create a cloud profile for you.

Payments

The app is distributed as a one-time paid download through the App Store. It does not contain subscriptions, in-app purchases, paid upgrades, or account-based purchases.

Notifications

If the app offers reminders, they are scheduled locally on your device. Notification settings can be changed in iOS Settings at any time.

Disclaimer

This app is an independent reference and personal organization tool. It is not an official government, exam, legal, medical, emergency, weather, or safety service. Information, reminders, calculations, and logs are provided for personal convenience only and are not guaranteed to be complete, current, or accurate. Always follow official guidance and professional advice where applicable.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support channel listed on the App Store product page.

This policy applies to LifePass UK (com.lifepass.uk).
